📋 THE 5 ROUNDS — what each measures (click to expand)
R1
QUALITY
  • ROIC (25%) — return on invested capital
  • FCF Margin (20%) — cash conversion
  • Margin Stability (20%) — 5yr variance
  • Balance Sheet (20%) — debt/equity
  • Capital Efficiency (15%) — asset turnover
R2
GROWTH
  • Hist EPS CAGR (25%) — past 5yr actual
  • Fwd EPS CAGR (25%) — analyst consensus
  • FCF Growth (25%) — cash compounding
  • Rev Consistency (15%) — growth stability
  • Reinvestment (10%) — Buffett g = ROE × reinvest
R3
DURABILITY
  • Terminal P/E (30%) — fair value multiple
  • Peer P/E (20%) — vs sector
  • Network Effect (20%) — moat strength
  • Act II (20%) — next growth chapter
  • Secularity (10%) — tailwind industry
R4
10-YEAR POTENTIAL
  • 3-Engine Growth Blend (40%) — consensus + trailing + ROE
  • Hist Growth Durability (20%)
  • PE-Adjusted CAGR (30%) — real return after PE compression
  • Industry Growth (20%)
  • Industry Secularity (15%)
R5
COMBO (FINAL)
  • Best combined R1-R4 score
  • PE-Adjusted 10yr CAGR carries 50% weight
  • Valuation dislocation (analyst target vs PE math)
  • Determines the Champion of Champions
